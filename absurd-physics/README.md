# 🌀 Absurd Physics
*A collection of playful, mathematically serious explorations of intentionally unrealistic physics scenarios.*

This folder contains a series of short LaTeX “episodes,” each analyzing an absurd or impossible physical setup with real mathematical tools. The goal is equal parts humor, creativity, and genuine physics reasoning.

---

## 📚 Contents

### Episode 1 — *Infinite Flat World Gravity*
Files:
- `absurd-physics-episode-1-infinite-flat-world-gravity.tex`
- `absurd-physics-episode-1-infinite-flat-world-gravity.pdf`

Explores what gravity would look like in an infinite, flat world—an impossible geometry treated with real physical modeling.

---

### Episode 2 — *Constant Gravity Atmosphere*
Files:
- `absurd-physics-episode-2-constant-gravity-atmosphere.tex`
- `absurd-physics-episode-2-constant-gravity-atmosphere.pdf`

Investigates atmospheric structure under a perfectly constant gravitational field, ignoring realistic planetary constraints.
